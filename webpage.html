DC Joker

who is Joker?

  • The Joker is a supervillain appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics
  • In his comic book appearances, the Joker is portrayed as a criminal mastermind. The character is introduced as a psychopath with a warped, sadistic sense of humor.
  • The Joker possesses no superhuman abilities, instead he's using his expertise in chemical engineering to develop poisonous or lethal concoctions and thematic weaponry.

DC Batman

who is Batman?

  • The Batman is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics.
  • The character was created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, and debuted in the 27th issue of the comic book Detective Comics on March 30, 1939.
  • In the DC Universe continuity, Batman is the alias of Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist, and industrialist who resides in Gotham City.
